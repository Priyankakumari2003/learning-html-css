Hii I am Priyanka Kumari currently pursuing B.Tech In Information Technology from CUSAT University.I made this website as a part of my first task in my Internship which is just to know about HTML and CSS.This website focuses on some men and women of India whose whole life is inspiration forThis website is a Parallax website which is a type of website design that creates an illusion of depth by having background images move slower than foreground images as the user scrolls down the page.It has been used for over a decade now and is not just another passing web design trend. Parallax websites create the same optical illusion as in real life where background elements are often set to move at much slower speeds than foreground elements. Unlike other interactive web design techniques requiring click-triggered animation, parallax scrolling bursts to life as you scroll up and down the page.

Mother Teresa

The Mother of The World

Mother Teresa was a Christian missionary who spent her entire life in India as a nun. She was born on 26th August 1910 in Yugoslavia (now the Republic of Macedonia) and came to India as a nun at the age of 19. She founded the Missionaries of Charity in 1950 to help the sick, the homeless, and the dying. She was a woman of compassion, humility, and faith who inspired millions of people with her message of humanity. To know more visit:importantindia.com

Kalpana Chawla

First Indian Women Space Traveller

Kalpana Chawla was an Indian-born American astronaut and aerospace engineer who was the first woman of Indian origin to fly to space. She first flew on Space Shuttle Columbia in 1997 as a mission specialist and primary robotic arm operator1. Unfortunately, during her second space flight, her space shuttle burst, and she lost her life and seven other astronauts in the year 20032.Her life and achievements continue to inspire and influence generations. To know more visit:space.com
